<p><strong>The Galvanized Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-galvanizing Wire</li><li>Hot-dip galvanized Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized wire market comprises two primary types: electro-galvanizing wire and hot-dip galvanized wire. Electro-galvanizing wire is coated with a thin layer of zinc through an electroplating process, offering a smooth finish and good corrosion resistance, suitable for light-duty applications. In contrast, hot-dip galvanized wire involves immersing the wire in molten zinc, resulting in a thicker, more robust coating that provides superior corrosion protection, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications in industries like construction and agriculture.</p></p>
