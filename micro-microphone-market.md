<p><p>The micro microphone market includes two primary types: ECM (Electret Capacitor Microphones) and MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) microphones. ECMs are widely used for their high sensitivity and low noise, making them ideal for consumer electronics like smartphones and cameras. In contrast, MEMS microphones are smaller and offer enhanced durability, making them suitable for applications in wearables and IoT devices. Both types cater to the growing demand for compact, high-performance audio solutions in various industries, including telecommunications and automotive.</p></p>
