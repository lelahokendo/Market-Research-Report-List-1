<p><p>Manufacturing CRM software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CRM solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced maintenance costs. These systems support real-time collaboration and data sharing across multiple locations. On-premises CRM software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control and customization at the cost of higher maintenance and infrastructure requirements. Each type serves different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
