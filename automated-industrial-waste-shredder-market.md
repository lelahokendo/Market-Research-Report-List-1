<p><p>The automated industrial waste shredder market comprises several types based on the configuration of cutting shafts. Single shaft shredders feature one rotating shaft, ideal for processing materials like plastics and wood with uniform particle size. Two shaft shredders have two parallel shafts, providing efficient shredding for a variety of materials, including metals and rubber. Four shaft shredders offer enhanced control and finer output sizes, suitable for hazardous waste. Other categories may include specialty or mobile shredders for specific applications.</p></p>
