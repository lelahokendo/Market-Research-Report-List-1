<p><ul><li>Non Interlock Tile</li><li>Interlock Tile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber tile market is categorized into two primary types: non-interlock tiles and interlock tiles. Non-interlock tiles are large, flat pieces that require adhesive for installation, making them suitable for permanent flooring solutions in areas like gyms and outdoor spaces. In contrast, interlock tiles feature a patented edge design that allows them to snap together easily, providing a versatile flooring option that can be quickly installed or rearranged. Both types offer durability, safety, and comfort for various applications.</p></p>
