<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wood Based Flooring Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particleboard (PB)</li><li>Medium density fibreboard (MDF)</li><li>High density fibreboard (HDF)</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li><li>Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood-based flooring panels include various types, each suited for different applications. Particleboard (PB) is made from wood particles and is economical but less durable. Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) offers a smooth finish and is ideal for cabinetry. High Density Fibreboard (HDF) is denser and more resistant to wear, making it popular for laminate floors. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) is engineered from strands and offers strength and moisture resistance. Plywood consists of layers of wood veneer, providing structural stability and versatility for flooring solutions.</p></p>
