<p><strong>The Low-E Coated Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Online Pyrolytic Deposition</li><li>Offline Vacuum Sputtering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low-E coated glass market is primarily categorized into two types based on the deposition methods: online pyrolytic deposition and offline vacuum sputtering. Online pyrolytic deposition involves applying the Low-E coating during the glass production process, resulting in a durable and cost-effective product suitable for various architectural applications. In contrast, offline vacuum sputtering occurs post-production, allowing for more precise control over coating properties, enhancing performance in specific applications like energy-efficient windows and high-end buildings.</p></p>
