<p><p>The biomass steam turbine market encompasses various types, including condensing, back pressure, and others. Condensing turbines maximize energy efficiency by converting steam to water, using lower temperatures to produce additional energy. Back pressure turbines, on the other hand, utilize steam to generate power while allowing excess steam for heating processes, providing operational flexibility. Other types may include extraction turbines, which can pull off steam for intermediate heating, catering to diverse industrial applications and enhancing energy management in biomass utilization.</p></p>
