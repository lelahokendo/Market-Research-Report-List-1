<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) lamps market is primarily divided into two types: UV mercury lamps and UV LED lamps. UV mercury lamps, which utilize mercury vapor to produce UV light, are commonly used in applications like water purification and industrial disinfection due to their high intensity and efficiency. In contrast, UV LED lamps offer advantages such as lower energy consumption, longer lifespan, and instant on-off capabilities. They are increasingly favored in applications like curing processes and sterilization due to their versatility and environmental benefits.</p></p>
