<p><strong>The UV Curing Printing Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Curing</li><li>LED Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV curing printing ink market primarily consists of two types: arc curing and LED curing. Arc curing utilizes high-intensity ultraviolet lamps to quickly cure inks, offering a broad spectrum of UV light for efficient drying on various substrates. In contrast, LED curing employs energy-efficient LEDs, providing controlled wavelength output and lower heat generation, making it suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Both technologies enhance print quality, reduce solvent emissions, and improve production speeds in various printing applications.</p></p>
