<p><p>The veterinary anesthesia machine flowmeter market is primarily segmented into single tube and multi-tube flowmeters. Single tube flowmeters allow for the measurement of oxygen or anesthetic gases through a single channel, offering simplicity and ease of use for smaller clinics. In contrast, multi-tube flowmeters feature multiple channels, enabling simultaneous monitoring and administration of various gases, making them ideal for larger veterinary practices or specialized facilities. Both types enhance precision and safety in delivering anesthesia during veterinary procedures.</p></p>
