<p><p>The industrial machinery brakes market primarily features two types: drum brakes and disc brakes. Drum brakes consist of a cylindrical drum that encases brake shoes; they provide effective braking force and are commonly used in heavy machinery. Disc brakes, on the other hand, utilize a rotating disc and brake calipers for more efficient heat dissipation, offering superior performance in high-speed applications. Both brake types are essential for ensuring safety and operational efficiency in industrial settings.</p></p>
