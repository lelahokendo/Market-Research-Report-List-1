<p><p>Actuation accessories encompass various market types based on their operational mechanisms. Pneumatic actuation relies on compressed air, offering fast response times and low maintenance. Hydraulic systems utilize pressurized fluid for powerful movements, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Electro-hydraulic combines electrical and hydraulic elements, allowing for precise control in complex systems. Electric actuation uses electric motors for direct movement, providing high efficiency and easy integration with automation systems. Each type serves distinct industries based on specific performance needs and application requirements.</p></p>
