<p><p>The touchless flush toilet market primarily includes two types of sensors: radar sensors and infrared sensors. Radar sensors utilize microwave technology to detect motion, automatically triggering a flush when a user approaches or moves away. In contrast, infrared sensors employ light waves to identify presence, activating the flush when a user is detected. Both types enhance hygiene and convenience in public restrooms and residential settings by minimizing contact, thus decreasing the spread of germs and improving overall sanitation.</p></p>
