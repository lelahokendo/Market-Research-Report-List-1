<p><p>The Diagnostic and Monitoring Ophthalmic Devices market encompasses various technologies used for eye examinations and disease management. Ophthalmic Ultrasound Imaging Systems utilize sound waves to create images of the eye's internal structures. Optical Coherence Tomographers offer high-resolution cross-sectional images to diagnose conditions like glaucoma and macular degeneration. Fundus Cameras capture detailed photographs of the retina, while Tonometers measure intraocular pressure. Ophthalmoscopes allow for a direct view of the retina, and Slit Lamps provide detailed examination of anterior eye structures. Other devices include additional diagnostic tools and portable instruments.</p></p>
