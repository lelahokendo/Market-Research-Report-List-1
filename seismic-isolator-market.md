<p><p>The seismic isolator market comprises several types, including Natural Rubber Bearings (NRB), Lead Rubber Bearings (LRB), High-Damping Rubber Bearings (HDR), and other variants. NRB allows buildings to flex during seismic events, reducing stress. LRB incorporates lead cores for added damping, enhancing energy dissipation. HDR utilizes high-damping materials to absorb vibrations effectively. Other isolators may include sliding bearings or specialized designs. Each type offers distinct advantages for earthquake resilience, catering to various structural needs and engineering requirements.</p></p>
