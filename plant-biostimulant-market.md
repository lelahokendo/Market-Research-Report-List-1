<p><p>The plant biostimulant market is primarily categorized into natural and biosynthetic types. Natural biostimulants are derived from organic sources such as plant extracts, animal waste, and microorganisms, enhancing plant growth and resilience without harmful chemicals. In contrast, biosynthetic biostimulants are manufactured through chemical processes to mimic natural compounds, offering targeted benefits like improved nutrient uptake and stress resistance. Both types aim to enhance crop productivity and sustainability in agricultural practices while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
