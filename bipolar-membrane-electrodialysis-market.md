<p><p>The Bipolar Membrane Electrodialysis (BMED) market is segmented into two types: two-compartment and three-compartment systems. Two-compartment systems consist of an anode and a cathode chamber separated by a bipolar membrane, allowing simultaneous ion transport and chemical reactions for various applications. In contrast, three-compartment systems have an additional compartment for improved separation of products and enhanced efficiency. This design enables better control of processes, making it suitable for more complex applications such as desalination and resource recovery.</p></p>
