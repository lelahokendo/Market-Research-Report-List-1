<p><p>The commercial vehicle parts magnesium die casting market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. **Pressure Die Casting** involves injecting molten magnesium into a mold under high pressure, ensuring precise and complex shapes. </p><p>2. **Vacuum Die Casting** utilizes a vacuum to minimize air pockets, enhancing the quality of the final product. </p><p>3. **Squeeze Die Casting** applies pressure to solidify the metal quickly, improving density and strength. </p><p>4. **Gravity Die Casting** relies on gravity to fill molds, making it suitable for larger components with simpler geometries. Each method caters to specific production requirements and material properties.</p></p>
