<p><strong>The Plunger Pin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Plunger</li><li>Ball Plunger</li><li>Cam-Action Plunger</li><li>Detent Plunger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plunger pin market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Threaded plungers feature a screw mechanism for adjustable depth, ideal for securing components. Ball plungers utilize a spring-loaded ball for quick engagement and release, suitable for quick connect/disconnect scenarios. Cam-action plungers provide a user-friendly, quick-action locking mechanism, useful in production setups. Detent plungers incorporate a mechanical detent mechanism to hold positions firmly, making them perfect for precision work. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, contributing to diverse market demand.</p></p>
