<p><strong>The Aircraft Deicing Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li><li>Type IV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft deicing fluids market comprises three primary types: Type I, Type II, and Type IV. Type I fluids are glycol-based, heated, and typically used for deicing, offering a short-lived protective layer. Type II fluids are also glycol-based but have a higher viscosity, providing longer-lasting protection during flight, and are primarily used on slower aircraft. Type IV fluids have even higher viscosity and are designed for use on faster aircraft, maximizing aerodynamic effectiveness and optimizing performance during takeoff by minimizing ice accumulation.</p></p>
