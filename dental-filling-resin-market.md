<p><p>The dental filling resin market comprises several types, including composite fillings, amalgam fillings, glass ionomer fillings, and others. Composite fillings are made of a tooth-colored resin, providing aesthetic appeal and versatility for small to medium cavities. Amalgam fillings are durable metal-based solutions, ideal for back teeth due to their strength. Glass ionomer fillings release fluoride and bond chemically to the tooth, suitable for areas needing decay protection. Other fillings may include materials like resin ionomers or temporary fillers, catering to diverse dental needs.</p></p>
