<p><strong>The PM2.5 Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TEOM Monitor</li><li>Beta Attenuation Monitor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PM2.5 monitoring equipment encompasses various types designed to measure particulate matter with a diameter of 2.5 micrometers or less. The TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) monitor uses a tapered element to provide real-time measurements of PM2.5 mass. Beta Attenuation Monitors function by measuring the attenuation of beta radiation as it passes through PM2.5 particles on a filter. Other types include optical monitors and gravimetric devices, which utilize alternative methods to assess air quality and particulate concentrations effectively.</p></p>
