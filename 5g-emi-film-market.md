<p><p>The 5G EMI film market comprises several types, including metal-mesh films and coating films. Metal-mesh films utilize a fine metallic grid to provide electromagnetic interference shielding while maintaining transparency, making them ideal for high-speed communications. Coating films, on the other hand, are applied directly onto substrates to create a conductive surface that blocks EMI. Both types enhance device performance in 5G technology, ensuring signal integrity and minimizing interference, crucial for the rollout of advanced wireless networks.</p></p>
