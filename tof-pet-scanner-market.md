<p><p>The TOF-PET scanner market encompasses three primary types: PET scanners, PET-CT scanners, and PET-MRI scanners. PET scanners utilize positron emission tomography to provide metabolic imaging. PET-CT scanners combine PET with computed tomography, offering detailed anatomical and functional information. PET-MRI scanners integrate PET with magnetic resonance imaging, delivering high-resolution soft tissue imaging alongside metabolic data. Each type caters to varying clinical needs, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in oncology, cardiology, and neurological disorders, thereby driving advancements in medical imaging technology.</p></p>
