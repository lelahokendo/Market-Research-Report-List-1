<p><strong>The Liquid Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist</li><li>Negative Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid photoresist market is primarily divided into two types: positive photoresist and negative photoresist. Positive photoresist becomes soluble when exposed to light, enabling precise patterning in semiconductor manufacturing and microelectronics. This type is favored for its high resolution. Conversely, negative photoresist hardens upon exposure, making the unexposed areas soluble; this is advantageous for applications requiring robust structures. Both types are essential in the fabrication of integrated circuits, displays, and other advanced technologies, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
